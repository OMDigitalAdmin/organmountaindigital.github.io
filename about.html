🏜️ What Is Organ Mountain Digital?

A digital front porch for Las Cruces. A platform built to amplify underrepresented events, community voices, and local culture — no paywalls, no junk ads.

❌ The Problem

  • 📅 Community calendars are scattered and outdated
  • 🎨 Local creatives and small orgs struggle to be seen
  • 💸 Big platforms prioritize paid ads over real content

🌟 The Vision

We're building a community-centered media platform that provides:

  • 🗓️ A single hub for events in Doña Ana County
  • 📣 Visibility for small organizations and creators
  • 💖 Accessibility over profit

👋 Who’s Behind It?

Organ Mountain Digital was created by a U.S. military veteran, parent, and community tech educator in Las Cruces. Someone who saw the need — and built the thing.
